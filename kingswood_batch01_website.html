Our Journey

From Randles Hill
to the World

Twenty-five years ago, we walked out of the gates of Randles Hill as young men ready to take on the world. Today, we return as professionals, entrepreneurs, and fathers — but most importantly, as brothers.

"The bonds forged on that hill were not of circumstance, but of character. We were shaped by the same values, the same call to excellence — and that endures."

25 Years in Perspective

Our Milestones

2001

We walked out of Kingswood College, Kandy — the world at our feet, our motto in our hearts.

2006

5-Year reunion. First reconnection as young professionals finding our paths.

2011

10-Year get-together. Careers established, families growing, friendships unshaken.

2016

15-Year reunion. Brothers now spread across the globe, yet always Kingswoodians at heart.

2026

Silver Jubilee — our greatest gathering yet. 25 years, one brotherhood, one celebration.
